Exploring Architectural Wonders

The Colosseum, located in the heart of Rome, stands as a testament to the grandeur of ancient engineering. This magnificent amphitheater could hold up to 80,000 spectators, showcasing the brilliance of Roman architectural innovation.

Modern architecture embraces minimalism and sustainability, with structures like The Shard in London symbolizing contemporary design. Tall, sleek, and efficient, modern buildings push the boundaries of traditional construction methods.
